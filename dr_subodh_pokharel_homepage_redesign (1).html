About

A lifetime dedicated to human dignity and sexual wellness

Dr. Subodh Pokharel is a leading Senior Sexual Health Specialist with over 30 years of clinical experience in practice, education, and public awareness. He is the founder of Mata Manakamana Arogya Clinic and the Nepal Institute of Human Sexuality.

An accomplished author, poet, lyricist, and filmmaker, Dr. Pokharel has written multiple books on human sexuality and sexual medicine. As a former member of the Constituent Assembly of Nepal, he played a vital role in shaping the nation's constitution.
